About Us

Our club was founded in the early 1970's by students and faculty with a common goal: to climb Mt. Denali in Alaska. Their successful summit of the third most-prominent mountain on Earth set the bar for high for pursuit of adventure, safety, and life-changing experiences. The Mountaineers have expanded to encompass all realms of outdoor activities ranging from biking and skiing, to caving and climbing.

Membership info

Membership is requiered to go on any Mountaineers trips. Memberships are available per semester (30$) or per year(40$). Free gear rentals from our stock of gear is included with membership along with a CollegeOutside membership (disscounted gear for college students). Memberships are non-refundable. OSU Mountaineers reserve the right to revoke a membership at any time without reimbursement if club policy is violated.
